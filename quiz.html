The Coughing Major Millionaire Scandal from The Chichester Festival Theatre Production


A new comedy by James Graham Directed by Daniel Evans with Sean Linnen

In September 2001, the nation was gripped by a scandal that remains, to this day, a mystery.


Charles Ingram, aka the Coughing Major, and his accomplices duped the world’s richest and most popular TV quiz show - Who Wants to Be a Millionaire - out of £1,000,000.

Or did they? And if they did cheat, how did they really do it?

Following ITV's new three-part series, this critically acclaimed West End smash hit comedy uncovers new evidence and asks you, the audience, to decide: guilty or not guilty.
